# Business Goal

## Company

Workplace Inc. — an internal-communications and workplace-intelligence platform
connecting employees with company news, incidents, discussions, documents, and
AI-powered signal detection. The platform aggregates internal and external
feeds into a unified workplace experience.

## Objective

Track external signals that materially affect the workplace: technology shifts
that impact internal tooling, security advisories that affect infrastructure,
AI/ML advances that could enhance the product, open-source license changes
affecting dependencies, and industry regulation around workplace data privacy
and AI governance. Filter out unrelated tech news, product announcements from
non-competitors, and general consumer technology.
